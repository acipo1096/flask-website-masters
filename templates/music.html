About Me

Music has been part of my life since I can remember. It all started when I (badly) danced around my house to Beatles records. I was taking piano lessons at 6 years old. By 13, I was becoming a competent guitarist, learning everything I could from my favorite guitarist, Jimmy Page.

My musicianship has expanded a lot since then. I now play five instruments - guitar, piano, bass, drums, and organ - and I sing, too. I can play over 400 songs on the guitar. My favorite genre is rock and roll, but I also love the sounds of the blues, jazz, and flamenco. I've been fortunate to play with some fantastic musicians, and we all play for the love of the music.

I'm always looking for new places to play - contact me here if you need someone for your band or event!
